The Design Concept

Overview

FORMA Architects is a fictional boutique studio - built around an AI persona brief - designing ultra-modern luxury residences across Miami, Dubai, and the Greek Islands.

The site has one purpose: make a high-net-worth prospect feel that FORMA is the only firm they should trust with their dream home.

The Challenge

Firms at this caliber rely on word of mouth - and lose to competitors with sharper websites. The site had to earn trust from buyers aged 35–60 in the Gulf, Europe, and Latin America before they ever pick up the phone.

FULL WIDTH HERO

Uncompromising visual presence

The Solution

A six-page site built like a magazine, not a brochure. Quiet luxury done with restraint - every detail earned.

  • Editorial portfolio hierarchy Hero projects display large; supporting work stays quiet. A magazine spread, not a uniform grid.
  • Immersive case studies Each project opens into a dedicated page with full-bleed photography and a consultation CTA at peak emotional engagement.
  • Dark CTA moment One full-width dark section that forces a pause: "spaces of silence and light that belong to you and the land."
  • Qualifying contact form No Calendly. A refined form with budget tiers ($2M to $15M+) that pre-qualifies leads on-brand.
  • The gold rule The brass accent B8956A appears only on hover, CTAs, and key moments - so when it shows, it feels earned.

Results

Six pages and individual case studies. Three iterations to land the typography scale and the gold rule. A site that communicates quiet confidence - no gradients, no stock handshakes, nothing loud.
